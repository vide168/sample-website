The Foundry 3 - architecturally designed commercial workspaces, ultra modern and flexible to suit your needs.
The workspaces can be used for trades as well as warehousing but will also be ideal for e-retailers, logistics providers, entrepreneurs, and start-ups. High studs of 6m and roller doors provide easy access for trucks, forklifts and commercial vehicles.
Each work space features high-speed fibre internet connectivity, a bathroom and at least one car park with every unit with CCTV covering the entire development.

Select a unit with mezzanine-floor office up-front, or opt for the flexibility to add your own mezzanine later to your own or your tenant’s specifications. The units are space efficient and flexible, so you can customise them to your requirements.
A design-led approach is another factor which sets The Foundry 3 apart from typical commercial and industrial property developments. Predominantly concrete tilt slab construction will keep maintenance to a minimum and a distinctive look by BSW Architects ensures The Foundry 3 will appeal to a range of businesses.
